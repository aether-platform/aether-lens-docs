## Accelerate Your Development

"Code changes shouldn't just run tests; they should reveal insights."

What determines the speed of development is the time spent "waiting for confirmation." Aether Lens minimizes them, allowing you to stay in your most creative moments.

### ⚡️ Next-Gen Feedback Loop

The essence of Aether Lens lies in its **tight integration with Vibe Coding Agents**. By instantly validating agent-generated code and providing insights, it creates a fully autonomous development cycle.

1. **Integrated UI Insights**: While you Vibe Code, Lens continuously checks the UI in the background, providing instant summaries. These insights can be immediately fed back into your next Vibe Coding iteration.
2. **Context-Aware Insights**: Goes beyond surface-level errors to provide corrective advice aligned with your project's "vibes" and design philosophy.
3. **Unified QA Platform**: QA teams can leverage the exact same environment as developers. By unifying development and test automation on one platform, delivery speed is dramatically increased.
4. **Zero Configuration**: Just run `aether-lens init`, and your AI agent gains "eyes" to become a partner responsible for quality.
